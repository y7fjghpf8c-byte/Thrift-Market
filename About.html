Thrift Market is a small business in the heartland of limpopo,polokwane.Established in 2024 by Tshupetso Rampedi,all in the aim of showcasing second hand clothing.over the years it has provided a user friendly digital experience allowing customers to navigate and view reusable clothes.

The Market has promoted sustainable fashion by encouraging thrifting(The practice of buying second hand items,instead of purchasing new ones)while maintaining quality and style.the business envisions a reliable and stable platform providing a seamless online thrifiting experience.

Since its instatement it has cultivated the south africa fashion industry becoming a huge pioneer of reusable clothing.
