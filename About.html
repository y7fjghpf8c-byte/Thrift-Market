Tshupetso Rampedi Founder:


Thrift Market is a small business in the heartland of Limpopo, Polokwane. Established in 2024 by Tshupetso Rampedi, all in the aim of showcasing second hand clothing. Over the years we have improved our online presence to help customers to navigate and view reusable clothes.

The Market has promoted sustainable fashion by encouraging thrifting (the practice of buying second hand items instead of purchasing new ones) while maintaining quality and style, and providing a seamless online thrifting experience.

Since its inception it has supported the South Africa fashion industry and grown as a pioneer of reusable clothing.
