# Thrift-Market


## Organisation overview:
Name: Thrift Market (fictional)
## Provide a brief history of the organisation
The initiative was initiated based on the increased price of fashion items, where Mr. Rampedi wanted to create a space for growth of second-hand fashion to thrive while retaining quality and affordability. Ever since its establishment. Thrift Market has made great progress and grown considerably to be a considerable part of the fashion industry locally.
## Mission and Vision statement
This business venture will revolve around sustainable fashion through the promotion of reusing and recycling clothes while maintaining high standards of quality and style at all times. This business aims to establish a consistent and dependable online thrifting platform for clothing that can be accessed conveniently through the website. (Williams, 2024)
## Target Audience
Individuals who are passionate about second-hand fashion and sustainability but want to express their unique identities and personalities.
## Website goals and objectives:
## Define specific goals of the website
It is important to create a user-friendly interface which will allow customers to easily find and buy used clothes, while simultaneously making use of the thrifting shop atmosphere which integrate into the effectiveness of current online shopping platforms.
## Key Performance Indicators(KPIs) 
(Team S. F., 2025)
-	Gross Profit Margin
-	Inventory Turnover rate
-	Average Transaction Value(ATV)
-	Donation Conversion Rate
-	Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

## Proposed Website Features and Functionality
-	Home: Navigation menu and promotional banners 
-	About us: Organisation history, mission, vision and team information
-	Services: Items of clothing available 
-	Contact: Contact information, location Map and enquiry form
-	Schedule: Operating hours

## Design and User Experience
Webflow (2025) This design will be fresh and modern, using the combination of innovation and creativity to creating an appealing design. Colour and good images will be used to make the site readable and user-friendly. Sections will be properly segmented, and there will be an increased focus on the presentation of products. navigation Menus will be located in the header of the page, with additional features appearing in the footer.
